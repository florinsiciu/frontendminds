I am Florin Siciu, and I built FrontendMinds because I kept having the same conversation with engineering leaders who were stuck.

They knew their Angular application needed modernization. They could see the build times climbing, the hiring getting harder, the technical debt compounding. But every time they tried to plan a path forward, they ran into the same wall: generic advice from people who had never actually done it at scale.

Over the past four years, I have led the modernization of 19 enterprise Angular applications. I migrated codebases from Angular 9 through 17, built 13 shared component libraries across micro-frontend architectures, rewrote three legacy AngularJS applications to modern Angular, and cut CI/CD pipeline times from over an hour to 20-30 minutes. I have seen what works, what fails, and what the difference looks like from inside the project, not from a blog post written by someone who read the documentation.

FrontendMinds is where that experience becomes practical, shareable knowledge.

## What You Will Find Here

This site focuses on three areas where I see the biggest gaps between what teams need and what content exists:

**Angular Modernization for Decision-Makers.** Most migration content is written for developers. But the people who approve budgets, set timelines, and allocate teams are CTOs, engineering managers, and tech leads. They need content that speaks their language: risk, cost, timeline, and ROI. That is what I write.

**AI Integration for Frontend Teams.** AI coding tools are transforming development, but most teams are adopting them without a governance framework. Which tools work for Angular? How do you measure productivity gains? What guardrails should you set? These are the questions I explore.

**Practical Frameworks, Not Theory.** I built the 5-Dimension Angular Modernization Framework because I needed a structured way to assess projects before recommending a migration approach. It evaluates Migration Health, Codebase Architecture, Modern Angular Adoption, AI Governance, and Delivery Readiness. That framework is now available as a free self-serve assessment that takes under three minutes.

## The Free Assessment

The Angular Modernization Assessment is the core tool on this site. Twenty yes-or-no questions across all five dimensions give you a personalized score, a diagnosis of your highest-risk areas, and concrete next steps. No sales call required, no email gate on the questions themselves.

I built it because every consulting engagement I have ever done started with the same diagnostic process. The assessment automates that first conversation so you can walk into your next planning meeting with data instead of assumptions.
